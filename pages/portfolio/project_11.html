Precision Lighting

This was a logo design project for light bulb manufacturer. The new company was new but wanted the name, look and feel to be like an older company.

Precision Lighting and Transformer is kind of wordy and the logo needed to work in a variety of applications including being printed on lightbulbs. I ultimately created multiple versions to address the multiple applications.
