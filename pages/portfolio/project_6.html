BAG Corp

BAG Corp is an industrial manufacturing company that has benefited from some really great branding. I was able to pull from this to create a really exciting site for what could been a really dry subject.

The clients products are highly technical and their audience is there to see the data. So information was king in this site design. Displaying the information and in a digestible manner as well as organizing the products were the major hurdles.

The resulting site really showcases everything in a way that is easy to read, looks great and still has all of the details needed to relay the technical aspects of the product lines.
