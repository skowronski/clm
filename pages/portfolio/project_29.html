Roomkey.com Homepage Re-design

Roomkey.com, online hotel booking, was switching over to a responsive framework. Part of that project included the resdesing of the company's homepage. I worked with Room Key to create a design that would easier to use than the original and create marketing space to accomodate the companies needs.

The resulting design is can accomodate mobile and tablet states, has ample marketing space and strongly emphasises the search function.
