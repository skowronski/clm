Duke Inc.

This project was the design of a website for Duke Inc. a construction company that builds large scale apartment complexes.

The site serves as a platform to show off past projects to prospective clients.

Despite being an established company that has been around for decades, this is really the beginnings of their presence on the web. The site was therefor designed to grow and their online portfolio grew and their needs for the site change.
