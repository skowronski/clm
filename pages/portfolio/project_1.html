The Perfect Last Bite

This project was it blog design for a very talented local chef and food stylist. She wanted to re-create the look of chalkboard menu drawings that many restaurants use. Just like the menu changes constantly a blog is constantly adding content so the blackboard theme really fits.

The goal was to also have the site ticket backseat the great photography and writing of the site. Therefore the design is really minimal.

This site was done on WordPress.
