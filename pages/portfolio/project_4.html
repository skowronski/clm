1814 Magazine

This project was the design of website for 1814 Magazine, a quarterly publication featuring the works of prominent fashion photographers. Like the magazine, the client wanted the site to be a vessel to showcase the photography and not distract from it.

The starkness of the site matches the magazine and has an almost gallery like feel.

As the site grows I am helping keep this feeling yet accomplish new goals like selling the magazine onsite, supporting contests and attracting advertisers.
